Crystal Game

You will be given a random number at the stat of the game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your totoal score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each crystal is hidden until you click on it.
Each time the game starts the game will change the values of the cystals.
